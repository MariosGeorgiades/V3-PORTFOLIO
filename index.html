01 — About

Who's behind the keyboard

I'm Marios — a recent high-school graduate from Strovolos, Nicosia, heading into a year of national service before reading Computer Science at university (AI & Networking specialisation).

I'm a three-time Cyprus Olympiad of Informatics competitor (national final in 2025, 100 pts), EJOI selection runner-up, Cisco CCNA × 2 certified, and an A-Level Further Mathematics — Grade A student with IELTS 7.5. I founded Ascend — a live motivational web platform used by students during exam season — and was the Web Developer & Event Technician at FX Sounds earlier this year — built their official site and ran AV at live events across Cyprus.

By evening I'm on the trail or in the gym. Cyprus Backyard Ultra finisher (70 km / 8 hrs), 4 years of Judo, 9 years of scouting (Democracy Award recipient). The willingness to sit with a hard problem long after it stops being fun — that's the through-line.
